Projects

One thing about me is I am always looking to try new things. This has been pretty handy in my life. If something breaks or is not working properly I will always try to fix it on my own before I go and pay to have someone fix it. Reason being is because I know I can do almost anything anyone else can if I really put my mind to it. So I wait until I have the motivation and time to attack a project and then I work on it. So far anything I have done I have completed. I may stumble along the way and lose motivation but one way or another I get the project done. Below I will post pictures and small stories of "Projects" my friends and I have taken up.
